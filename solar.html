The Solar System

The solar system is made up of the Sun and all the celestial objects that orbit it. This includes planets, moons, asteroids, comets, and more.

Planets in Our Solar System

1.Mercury - The smallest planet and closest to the Sun.
2. Venus - Similar in size to Earth but with a toxic atmosphere.
3. Earth - Our home planet, the only one known to support life.
4. Mars - The Red Planet, with potential for future human missions.
5. Jupiter - The largest planet, known for its Great Red Spot.
6. Saturn - Famous for its stunning ring system.
7. Uranus - An ice giant with a unique sideways rotation.
8. Neptune - The farthest known planet, with supersonic winds.
